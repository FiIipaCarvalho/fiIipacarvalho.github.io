NOC Glider Toolbox

GLIDERS I / GLIDERS II

Processes raw glider data to science-ready quality level in OG1 format. Developed as part of the GLIDERS infrastructure programme to make NOC glider observations FAIR-compliant and ready for submission to international data archives.
