Research Projects

Current and past research initiatives

Current Projects

ReBELS

Resolving Biological carbon Export in the Labrador Sea

2023-2028 • NERC Standard Grant, £1.05M • Co-PI, WP1-2 lead

Understanding how winter convection and spring restratification control biological carbon export in the Labrador Sea using autonomous platforms and ship-based observations.

POLOMINTS

Polar Ocean Mixing by Internal Tsunamis

2024-2029 • NERC Large Grant, £3.7M • Co-PI, WP2 lead

Investigating the role of internal waves and mixing in polar ocean biogeochemistry and carbon cycling.

PARTITRICS

PARTIcle Transformation and Respiration Influence on ocean Carbon Storage

2023-2026 • NERC BIO-Carbon Programme, £2.3M • Co-PI, WP1 lead

Quantifying particle transformation processes and their impact on carbon export efficiency in the twilight zone.

IDAPro

Integrating Drivers of Atlantic Productivity

2023-2026 • NERC BIO-Carbon Programme, £2.3M • Co-PI, NOC PI

Integrating physical and biological drivers of productivity across Atlantic Ocean regions to understand carbon export variability.

GLIDERS II

GLider-type Infrastructure and Digital Environment for Research Science

2026-2027 • FMRI, £250k • PI

Expanding glider capabilities and observing networks for ecosystem monitoring.

GLIDERS I

GLider-type Infrastructure and Digital Environment for Research Science

2025-2026 • Internal funding (RISC), £167k • PI

Developing glider infrastructure and data pipelines for sustained ocean observation.

APART

Autonomous Platforms as A Research Tool

2025-2026 • UKRI, £44k • PI

Training and capacity building for autonomous platform deployment and data analysis.
